SERVICES WE PROVIDE

At Dublin cycles we cater for all of your cycling needs

Repairs/ Servicing

Free Deliveries

Cycle Insurance

  • Many home contents policies will not cover items like cycles if they are over a certain value or they are taken away from the home.If you take your cycles out at the weekends, they may also not be covered if you transport them on a car rack
  • For Accident and Compensation insurance claims, either fill in contact form at the bottom of the website or call Deano for details.

Police Security

  • At Dublin Cycles, we offer a FREE security registration to help protect your bikes.
  • Just pop into our store with your Old or New bike and proof of ownership for the free registration. The information will then be uploaded onto a police database.
