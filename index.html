SERVICES WE PROVIDE

At Dublin cycles we cater for all of your cycling needs

Repairs/ Servicing

  • - First free service with every bicycle purchased from us within 6 weeks
  • - Same day repairs
  • - No repair is too difficult to handle
  • - Subsequent servicing of your bicyle is also available.Ask in store for details/price.

Free Deliveries

  • - Free delivery for locations within a 0.5 mile radius from the shop
  • - Deliveries beyond this radius will be priced accordingly upon request
  • - We do not deliver overseas

Cycle Insurance

  • - Many home contents policies will not cover items like cycles if they are over a certain value or they are taken away from the home.If you take your cycles out at the weekends, they may also not be covered if you transport them on a car rack
  • - For Accident and Compensation insurance claims, either fill in contact form at the bottom of the website or call Deano for details.

Police Security

  • - At Dublin Cycles, we offer a FREE security registration to help protect your bikes.
  • - Just pop into our store with your Old or New bike and proof of ownership for the free registration. The information will then be uploaded onto a police database.
  • - Contact our store for more details

A Little bit about us and our offers

From time to time, especially during Easter and Xmas, be sure to check out our offers here.

Dublin Cycles is an independent bicycle shop established well over a decade and a member of the Association of Cycle Traders. We pride ourselves on offering first class customer service, price and products to all our customers.

We have over 35 years experience in the cycle trade. Our sales staff are qualified cycle technicians with immense knowledge and we are passionate about the products we sell.

We are associated as members of the following Government schemes: Cyclescheme, Bike2work, NHS Cycle to work, The Salary Exchange, On Your Bike and WorkRiders.
