A Little bit about us and our offers

From time to time, especially during Easter and Xmas, be sure to check out our offers here.

Dublin Cycles is an independent bicycle shop established well over a decade and a member of the Association of Cycle Traders. We pride ourselves on offering first class customer service, price and products to all our customers.

We have over 35 years experience in the cycle trade. Our sales staff are qualified cycle technicians with immense knowledge and we are passionate about the products we sell.

We are associated as members of the following Government schemes: Cyclescheme, Bike2work, NHS Cycle to work, The Salary Exchange, On Your Bike and WorkRiders.
